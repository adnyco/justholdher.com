Agent Snapshot

Just Hold Her is a completed 85,000-word literary memoir at the intersection of foster care, medical complexity, and the ethics of daily caregiving. Built from contemporaneous notes kept through long nights, it moves from fear to fierce attachment… and finally, to letting go. A witness story, not a rescue story.
