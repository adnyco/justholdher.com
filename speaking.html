Bring This Story to Your Community

They were asked to take in a baby not expected to survive.

“Would you, please… just hold her until she dies.”

What followed was not dramatic. It was routine.

Every two hours. Through the night. For months.

Just Hold Her is a story about the quiet work of caregiving,
and what it means to stay.

Judy offers these visits to communities where this kind of work
is already understood… or quietly lived.

Judy Wright

Foster Parent · Author

Over twelve years, Judy and her husband, John, fostered fifty-five children, many of them medically fragile. They were recognized as Michigan Foster Parents of the Year in 2012.

She now lives in Raleigh, North Carolina.
